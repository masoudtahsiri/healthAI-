## Description

Transform your health data into actionable insights with AI-powered analysis, all processed privately on your device.

HealthAI+ uses Apple Intelligence™ on supported devices to provide personalised health insights. On devices without Apple Intelligence, the app uses Groq AI for AI analysis—your data is processed securely and never stored or used for tracking.

**AI-POWERED INSIGHTS**

• Personalised health analysis powered by Apple Intelligence™ (on supported devices) or Groq AI
• Pattern recognition to identify trends in your fitness journey
• Smart recommendations for workouts, nutrition, and recovery
• Comprehensive analysis of your health metrics over time

**COMPREHENSIVE HEALTH TRACKING**

• Seamless Apple Health® integration for all your health data
• Track workouts, steps, distance, and active calories
• Monitor heart rate, resting heart rate, and recovery metrics
• Analyse sleep patterns and quality
• View body composition changes and progress tracking

**PRIVACY-FIRST DESIGN**

• On-device processing with Apple Intelligence™ (iOS 26.0+ on A17 Pro+ devices)
• Secure AI processing with Groq (on older devices)—data is not stored or tracked
• Your health data stays private—no cloud storage, no data collection
• No tracking, no sharing, complete privacy and security

**BEAUTIFUL INTERFACE**

• Modern SwiftUI design with intuitive navigation
• Interactive charts and graphs for visual insights
• Personalised dashboard with your key metrics
• Customizable date ranges (weekly, monthly, yearly views)
• Smooth animations and responsive layouts

**KEY FEATURES**

• User profile management with fitness goals
• Real-time health data analysis
• Progress scoring and body composition predictions
• Workout efficiency insights
• Recovery recommendations
• Pattern analysis across all your health metrics
• Comprehensive recommendations covering workouts, nutrition, and lifestyle

**REQUIREMENTS**

Requires iOS 17.6+ or iPadOS 17.6+. For full Apple Intelligence™ features, requires iOS 26.0+ or iPadOS 26.0+ and a device with A17 Pro chip or newer. Optimised for iPhone® and iPad®.

**GET STARTED**

Simply connect your Apple Health APP® data and let HealthAI+ analyse your health patterns. The app provides personalised insights and recommendations to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Your health journey, powered by AI, protected by privacy.
